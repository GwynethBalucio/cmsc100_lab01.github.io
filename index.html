Favorite GE Subject

HIST 1

Philippine History

I never got to appreciate our country's history back in elementary. In college, HIST 1 gave me a better perspective on events that happened in the past. While giving us the opportunity to learn about our history in a critical and objective way, we were encouraged to humanize the people who experienced the past and not alienate them just because we often feel that they are different from the present. With that, I got to learn from this subject that the best way for us to do at present is to acknowledge our past and learn from it so we can afford a better security for our rights in the future.
